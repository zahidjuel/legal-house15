The Legal Practice Area

Legal practice refers to the profession of providing legal advice and representation to clients.The specific areas of practice can vary widely and may include areas such as criminal law, family law, immigration law, corporate law, and real estate law.

Business Law

Business law, also known as commercial law, is a broad area of legal practice that covers the legal issues related to the operation and regulation of businesses.

Criminal Law

Criminal law is a branch of law that deals with crimes and their punishment. It defines what behavior is considered a crime and sets out the penalties for committing such crimes.

Child Support

Child support is the financial assistance that is legally required to be provided by a parent for the care and maintenance of their child or children.

Education Law

Education law refers to the laws and regulations that govern the operation and administration of educational institutions, as well as the rights and responsibilities of students, parents, teachers, and school administrators.

Divorce Law

Divorce law in Bangladesh is governed by the Muslim Family Laws Ordinance, 1961. Under this law, Muslim couples can seek divorce through the traditional Islamic procedure of talaq (or "repudiation") in which a husband can divorce his wife by pronouncing the word "talaq" three times

Tax Law

Tax laws in Bangladesh are governed by the National Board of Revenue (NBR) which is the principal tax collection agency of the Government of Bangladesh

The purpose of a media query is to apply CSS styles based on the characteristics of the device or screen on which the website is being viewed. Media queries allow developers to create responsive designs that adapt to the screen size and resolution of the device being used. This means that the same HTML code can be used to display a website on a wide range of devices, such as desktop computers, laptops, tablets, and smartphones, while still providing an optimal viewing experience.

The CSS box model is a layout model used by web browsers to calculate the dimensions and position of elements on a web page.
